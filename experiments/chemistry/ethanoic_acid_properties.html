Properties of Ethanoic Acid

Theory

To study the physical and chemical properties of ethanoic acid (CH₃COOH), including its reaction with NaHCO₃, NaOH, and alcohol (esterification).

Procedure

  • Test smell and solubility of ethanoic acid in water.
  • Add ethanoic acid to NaHCO₃ solution — observe effervescence (CO₂).
  • Add ethanoic acid to NaOH — neutralization reaction.
  • Mix ethanoic acid + ethanol + few drops conc. H₂SO₄ — warm gently.
  • A fruity smell indicates ester (ethyl ethanoate) formation.

Apparatus

  • Test tubes
  • Bunsen burner
  • Ethanoic acid (glacial acetic acid)
  • NaHCO₃ solution
  • NaOH solution
  • Ethanol
  • Conc. H₂SO₄ (catalyst)
  • Litmus paper
