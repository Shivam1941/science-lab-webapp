Properties of Ethanoic Acid Experiment

Study physical and chemical properties of acetic acid including esterification.

Theory

Study physical and chemical properties of acetic acid including esterification. To study the physical and chemical properties of ethanoic acid (CH₃COOH), including its reaction with NaHCO₃, NaOH, and alcohol (esterification).

Formula

Principle: To study the physical and chemical properties of ethanoic acid (CH₃COOH), including its reaction with NaHCO₃, NaOH, and alcohol (esterification).

Aim

To study the physical and chemical properties of ethanoic acid (CH₃COOH), including its reaction with NaHCO₃, NaOH, and alcohol (esterification).

Apparatus

  • Test tubes
  • Bunsen burner
  • Ethanoic acid (glacial acetic acid)
  • NaHCO₃ solution
  • NaOH solution
  • Ethanol
  • Conc. H₂SO₄ (catalyst)
  • Litmus paper

Procedure

  1. Test smell and solubility of ethanoic acid in water.
  2. Add ethanoic acid to NaHCO₃ solution — observe effervescence (CO₂).
  3. Add ethanoic acid to NaOH — neutralization reaction.
  4. Mix ethanoic acid + ethanol + few drops conc. H₂SO₄ — warm gently.
  5. A fruity smell indicates ester (ethyl ethanoate) formation.
