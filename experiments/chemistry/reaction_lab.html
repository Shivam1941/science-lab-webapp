Chemical Reactions Lab Experiment

Observe different types of chemical reactions: combination, decomposition, displacement.

Theory

Observe different types of chemical reactions: combination, decomposition, displacement. To perform and observe different types of chemical reactions and classify them as combination, decomposition, displacement, or double displacement reactions.

Formula

Principle: To perform and observe different types of chemical reactions and classify them as combination, decomposition, displacement, or double displacement reactions.

Aim

To perform and observe different types of chemical reactions and classify them as combination, decomposition, displacement, or double displacement reactions.

Apparatus

  • Test tubes & test tube holder
  • Bunsen burner
  • Spatula
  • Chemicals: Iron filings, Copper sulfate solution, Zinc granules, Lead nitrate, Potassium iodide, Ferrous sulfate crystals

Procedure

  1. Combination: Heat iron filings in air → iron oxide forms.
  2. Decomposition: Heat ferrous sulfate crystals → colour change from green to brown.
  3. Displacement: Add zinc granules to copper sulfate solution → solution turns colourless.
  4. Double Displacement: Mix lead nitrate + potassium iodide → yellow precipitate of PbI₂.
