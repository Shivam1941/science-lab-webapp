Chemical Reactivity Workstation

Theory

To study the reactivity of metals (Zn, Fe, Cu, Mg) with dilute acids and to arrange them in order of reactivity.

Procedure

  • Add dilute HCl to each metal sample in separate test tubes.
  • Observe the rate of hydrogen gas evolution.
  • Test the gas with a burning splint (pop sound = H₂).
  • Record observations and arrange metals by reactivity.

Apparatus

  • Test tubes
  • Dilute HCl
  • Dilute H₂SO₄
  • Metal samples: Zinc, Iron, Copper, Magnesium
  • Delivery tube
  • Soap solution (for hydrogen testing)
