Respiration Lab Experiment

Demonstrate aerobic and anaerobic respiration using interactive simulations.

Theory

Demonstrate aerobic and anaerobic respiration using interactive simulations. To demonstrate that CO₂ is released during respiration by germinating seeds.

Formula

Principle: To demonstrate that CO₂ is released during respiration by germinating seeds.

Aim

To demonstrate that CO₂ is released during respiration by germinating seeds.

Apparatus

  • Conical flask
  • Germinating seeds (gram/pea)
  • KOH solution (to absorb CO₂)
  • Lime water (Ca(OH)₂)
  • Delivery tube
  • Cotton plug

Procedure

  1. Place germinating seeds in a conical flask.
  2. Connect the flask to a test tube containing lime water via delivery tube.
  3. Seal the flask with a cotton plug.
  4. Leave for 1-2 hours.
  5. Observe the lime water turning milky → confirms CO₂ release.
  6. Repeat with boiled (dead) seeds as control — no change in lime water.
