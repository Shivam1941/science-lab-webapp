Measurement of Pulse Rate

Theory

To measure the pulse rate at rest and observe the effect of different physical exercises on the pulse rate and recovery time.

Procedure

  • Ensure the subject is at rest and measure the resting pulse rate for 60 seconds.
  • Select the fitness level of the subject.
  • Choose an exercise (Running, Jumping Jacks, Skipping) and a duration.
  • Perform the exercise and immediately measure the post-exercise pulse rate.
  • Observe the pulse graph and record data in the observation table.
  • Monitor the recovery time until pulse returns to the resting rate.

Apparatus

  • Stopwatch / Timer
  • Human subject (interactive model)
  • Virtual Pulse Monitor
