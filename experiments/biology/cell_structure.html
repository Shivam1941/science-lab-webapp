Cell Structure Explorer Experiment

Explore animal and plant cell structures with interactive labelled diagrams.

Theory

Explore animal and plant cell structures with interactive labelled diagrams. To study and compare the structure of animal and plant cells, identifying their key organelles.

Formula

Principle: To study and compare the structure of animal and plant cells, identifying their key organelles.

Aim

To study and compare the structure of animal and plant cells, identifying their key organelles.

Apparatus

  • Microscope
  • Prepared slides of animal and plant cells
  • Drawing sheet and pencils

Procedure

  1. Place the plant cell slide under the microscope.
  2. Focus at low power (10x), then switch to high power (40x).
  3. Identify and draw: cell wall, cell membrane, nucleus, vacuole, chloroplasts.
  4. Repeat with the animal cell slide.
  5. Compare structures: note the absence of cell wall and chloroplasts in animal cells.
