Virtual Seed Dissection Experiment

Dissect and explore the internal structure of monocot and dicot seeds.

Theory

Dissect and explore the internal structure of monocot and dicot seeds. To study the external and internal structure of monocot (maize) and dicot (gram/bean) seeds through dissection.

Formula

Principle: To study the external and internal structure of monocot (maize) and dicot (gram/bean) seeds through dissection.

Aim

To study the external and internal structure of monocot (maize) and dicot (gram/bean) seeds through dissection.

Apparatus

  • Soaked gram seeds (dicot)
  • Soaked maize seeds (monocot)
  • Scalpel / Blade
  • Magnifying glass
  • Iodine solution
  • Drawing sheet

Procedure

  1. Soak seeds overnight in water.
  2. Observe external features: seed coat, hilum, micropyle.
  3. Carefully split the dicot seed — identify two cotyledons, radicle, plumule.
  4. Cut the monocot seed longitudinally — identify endosperm, single cotyledon (scutellum), coleoptile, coleorhiza.
  5. Apply iodine on endosperm — blue-black confirms starch.
  6. Draw labelled diagrams of both types.
