Plant Cell Explorer Experiment

Examine the structure of a plant cell with interactive labelled diagrams.

Theory

Examine the structure of a plant cell with interactive labelled diagrams. To prepare a temporary mount of an onion peel and observe plant cell structure under a microscope.

Formula

Principle: To prepare a temporary mount of an onion peel and observe plant cell structure under a microscope.

Aim

To prepare a temporary mount of an onion peel and observe plant cell structure under a microscope.

Apparatus

  • Microscope
  • Onion
  • Glass slides and cover slips
  • Forceps and needle
  • Safranin stain
  • Dropper and blotting paper
  • Watch glass

Procedure

  1. Peel a thin layer from the inner surface of an onion scale.
  2. Place the peel on a clean glass slide.
  3. Add a drop of safranin stain and wait 2 minutes.
  4. Place a cover slip gently, avoiding air bubbles.
  5. Observe under low power (10x) then high power (40x).
  6. Identify cell wall, cell membrane, nucleus, and vacuole.
