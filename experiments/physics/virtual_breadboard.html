Virtual Breadboard Experiment

Build series and parallel resistor circuits and calculate equivalent resistance.

Theory

Build series and parallel resistor circuits and calculate equivalent resistance. To study series and parallel combinations of resistors and calculate the equivalent resistance using a virtual breadboard.

Formula

Req = R₁ + R₂ + R₃

Aim

To study series and parallel combinations of resistors and calculate the equivalent resistance using a virtual breadboard.

Apparatus

  • Breadboard
  • Resistors (various values)
  • Battery / DC power supply
  • Ammeter and Voltmeter
  • Connecting wires
  • Multimeter

Procedure

  1. Select resistors from the component tray.
  2. Add resistors to the series rail — they are connected end-to-end.
  3. Add resistors to the parallel rail — they are connected across the same two points.
  4. Read the equivalent resistance from the multimeter display.
  5. Observe how current distributes in series vs parallel.
  6. Try the challenge: create 15Ω from three 10Ω resistors.
