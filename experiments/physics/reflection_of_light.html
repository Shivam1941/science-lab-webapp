Reflection of Light

Theory

To verify the laws of reflection: (1) angle of incidence = angle of reflection, and (2) the incident ray, reflected ray, and normal all lie in the same plane.

Procedure

  • Fix white paper on drawing board.
  • Draw a line and place the mirror on it.
  • Draw a normal (perpendicular) at the point of incidence.
  • Place two pins along the incident ray.
  • Looking from the other side, place two more pins such that all four appear in line.
  • Measure angle of incidence and angle of reflection with protractor.
  • Repeat for different angles.

Apparatus

  • Plane mirror
  • Protractor / Circular disc
  • Pins (drawing pins)
  • Drawing board
  • White paper
  • Scale / Ruler
